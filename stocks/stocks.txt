Stock Portfolio

Aggregates all the stock investments.


Stock Investment

Stores all investment operations regarding a single stock. It has information about the current number of shares, market
value, fees, past realizations, and dividends


Stock Operations

Represents a single operation of buying or selling shares. It has information about the stock, day, value (share, fees,
total), and number of shares.

The StockOperation is the base abstract class.

Attributes:
 - stock
 - day
 - shares
 - price
 - fees

Property:
 - total_value

Abstract:
 - is_buy

Others
 - __repr__

The buy and sell operations inherit the StockOperation and implement/override the following methods:
 - is_buy
 - __repr__
